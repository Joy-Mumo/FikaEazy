WHO WE ARE

FikaEazy is an easy, reliable website that provides adequate information about how to get to our users' preferred destinations with less hassle.

WHY FIKAEAZY?

After a very extensive survey, we realized most people find it very difficult to access accurate information about how to get to a place they have never been before. This leads to time wastage, spending more money and stress. Hence the need for FikaEazy, a website that is cost effective, time friendly and very easy to use.
